Our Focus


At Jibon Sohojiya Foundation, we are committed to empowering underprivileged communities by providing access to education, healthcare, and livelihood opportunities. We believe that every person deserves the chance to lead a dignified and fulfilling life, and we work tirelessly to make that a reality.

Sports Center

Our Sports Center offers a variety of facilities and programs for people of all ages and skill levels. From basketball and soccer to yoga and dance, we have something for everyone.

Through our sports programs, we aim to promote physical fitness and healthy lifestyles, as well as foster teamwork, leadership, and sportsmanship. Science Exploratorium

Our Science Exploratorium offers a variety of interactive exhibits and hands-on experiments that allow visitors to explore and discover the wonders of science in a fun and engaging way.

Through our exhibits and programs, we aim to inspire a love for science and encourage young minds to pursue careers in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics (STEM).

Education for Underprivileged People

As an NGO, our organization provides education to underprivileged people, which refers to individuals or communities who have limited access to educational opportunities due to various socioeconomic, cultural, or geographical factors. Your organization's goal is to bridge this educational gap by offering learning opportunities and resources that enable these individuals to gain knowledge, skills, and confidence needed to improve their lives and communities.

our organization provide formal or non-formal education programs, including primary and secondary education, vocational training, literacy programs, and skills development workshops. Additionally, our NGO may work closely with local communities, governments, and other organizations to create sustainable education systems and policies that address the root causes of educational disparities.

Rural Telemedicine

Our Rural Telemedicine program leverages technology to connect doctors and healthcare professionals with patients in remote and underserved areas.

Through our telemedicine services, we provide medical consultation to people living in rural areas where medical facilities are scarce. We have a team of experienced doctors and medical professionals who provide consultation to patients and offer them the necessary medical advice and treatment. Our telemedicine services have proved to be very helpful for people who have limited access to healthcare facilities.

Agriculture Supporting Farmers

Our NGO works towards promoting sustainable agriculture practices and providing support to farmers. We help farmers in adopting new farming techniques, providing them with the necessary equipment, and connecting them with the market to sell their produce. We believe that by supporting farmers, we can contribute towards building a sustainable and self-reliant community.

Livelihood

Our Livelihood Education program provides training and support to underprivileged individuals and communities to help them acquire the skills and knowledge necessary to earn a sustainable livelihood.

Through our training programs and support services, we aim to empower individuals and communities to break the cycle of poverty and achieve financial stability and independence.

Our Vision

We envision a world where every individual has access to basic necessities such as education, healthcare, and livelihood opportunities. We believe that by empowering communities and building futures, we can create a more equitable and just society where everyone has the opportunity to thrive.

A strong support system makes things happen.

We are an NGO dedicated to creating positive change in our community through various initiatives. Join us to support our mission and make a meaningful impact on the lives of those in need. Together, we can create a better world for all.
